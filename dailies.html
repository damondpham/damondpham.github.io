Dailies


- a series of digital images that change over time, each at its own pace

- something between an advent calendar, sundial, Dorian Gray's portrait, almanac, or a very very very long GIF

- honoring long-term thinking & renewability over excess & spam

- a counterpoint to Neopets "Dailies" and a certain flavor of NFT animations

- tokens are for sale on the Tezos blockchain

- around one per year is created

Viewing note: the file sizes are large, so on slower connections the images may render with hesitation or incompleteness. I usually have better luck viewing them on objkt.com (linked in the titles).
